Interactive Multi-Instrument Exhibit (Unity)

Partner: FORTH
Client: Museum of Anogeia

An interactive Unity installation that connects three physical instruments to a touchscreen controller and synchronized digital displays. Designed to create an immersive and educational museum experience, blending tactile interaction with digital storytelling.

Mirror Engine: Real-Time Motion Comparison Tool (Unity)

Partner: FORTH
Client: Museum of Anogeia

A real-time Unity tool for comparing humanoid animations with Kinect motion data. It integrates with a front-end app, database, and MinIO system to process user inputs, retrieve animation files and metadata, and generate detailed motion comparison results. Used in a dance training application to support both full-sequence and step-by-step performance analysis.

VR Treasure Hunt Exploring Historical Sites in Heraklion (Unity)

A VR treasure hunt set in a 3D recreation of Saint Mark’s Basilica, which serves as a central hub with eight doors leading to 360° scenes of Heraklion. Each location features interactive riddles that encourage exploration and learning, offering an immersive and educational journey through the city’s history.

Kastron That Grows (Unity)

Team Project
Created for Coco Code GameJam 2 (1 week)

A decision-based kingdom-management game where you play as the ruler of a distant island, tasked with constructing a towering monument while balancing the influence of three competing factions: the Greens, the Blues, and the Church. Each day, emissaries demand favors in exchange for helping build the tower, and every choice shifts the factions' power, letting any one faction fall to 0 or reach 100 leads to the collapse of your rule.

About Me

I’m a Software Engineer and Game Developer focused on creating gameplay that feels fluid, intentional, and expressive. My work spans Unity, Three.js, WebXR, and interactive 3D experiences, with projects ranging from immersive environments to interactive installations. I’m driven by the idea that mechanics, movement, and atmosphere should work together to shape how a world feels. Inspired by creators like Fumito Ueda, Hidetaka Miyazaki, and Shigeru Miyamoto, I aim to design experiences where simplicity, clarity, and emotion guide the player’s journey. As I continue toward the game development industry, I’m focused on growing as a gameplay and systems designer and building experiences that evoke curiosity, challenge, and a sense of discovery.
